Why We Started

We started The Pizza Place with a simple mission: to serve delicious, hand crafted pizza in a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Wheather you're getting take out or ordering delivery, we're here to bring people together - one pizza at a time.

Made with Love

We believe pizza is more than just food it's an experiance. Wheather you love a supreme pizza or a coustom creation piled with your favorite toppings we've got something for everyone. We Deliver!

At The Pizza Place, we know that life can be busy - but that doesn't mean you have to settle for anything less than amazing pizza! That's why we specialize in fast, fresh, and delicious take out that brings flavors you love straight to your table, wherever you are.
